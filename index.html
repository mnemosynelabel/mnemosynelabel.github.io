III
The Label

"built by those who refused to forget.
built for artists who make things that last."

MNEMOSYNE was built by two siblings who spent years making art inside systems that owned it.

They left. They built something where the artist controls everything — the sound, the image, the story, the rights.

Named after the Titan goddess of memory. Mother of the nine Muses. Origin of every creative discipline. Because art is just memory with the nerve to become something permanent.
